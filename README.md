# Ziva Uganda 🌟

### *Shine From Within*

Ziva Uganda is a Community Based Organization (CBO) dedicated to improving the health, dignity, and wellbeing of adolescent girls and young women in Uganda — through education, mentorship, and community engagement.

> **Ziva** means *radiance, brilliance, light.*

-----

## 🌍 About Us

Too many girls miss school because of period shame, lack of information, or lack of basic hygiene resources. Too many young people navigate puberty and health challenges without guidance.

**Ziva Uganda exists to change that.**

We stand at the intersection of health, dignity, faith, and youth empowerment — helping young people discover the confidence and dignity that already lives within them. Because when young people shine from within, communities shine too.

-----

## 🎯 Vision

> A generation of young people in Uganda who are healthy, confident, informed, and shining from within.

## 🚀 Mission

To improve the health, dignity, and wellbeing of young people — especially adolescent girls and young women — through education, access to services, mentorship, and community engagement.

## 🎪 Our Programs

### 🌸 Ziva School Health Clubs

Peer-led health education in schools addressing period poverty, menstrual stigma, and hygiene access — with dignity at the centre.

### 🎒 Ziva Dignity Kits

Menstrual hygiene kits distributed to girls, ensuring no student misses school because of a lack of basic resources.

### 💬 Ziva Conversations

Safe, youth-friendly discussions about puberty, reproductive health, mental health, confidence, and self-care — without shame.

### 🎓 Ziva Campus Ambassadors

University students trained to lead youth health advocacy in their communities, building the next generation of leaders.

### 🏘️ Community Health Outreach

Connecting schools and communities to health services and awareness programs that create lasting impact.

Who We Are

Born from a belief that young people deserve to shine

Ziva means radiance, brilliance, light. We stand at the intersection of health, dignity, faith, and youth empowerment.

Too many girls miss school because of period shame, lack of information, or lack of basic hygiene resources. Too many young people navigate puberty and health challenges without guidance.

Ziva Uganda was born to change that — through education, mentorship, and community engagement that helps young people discover the confidence and dignity that already lives within them.

Because when young people shine from within, communities shine too.

Our Vision

"A generation of young people in Uganda who are healthy, confident, informed, and shining from within."

Our Mission

To improve the health, dignity, and wellbeing of young people — especially adolescent girls and young women — through education, access to services, mentorship, and community engagement.
